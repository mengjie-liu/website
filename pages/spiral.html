Spiral
Next Project
website, installation, 2021
www.mengjieliu.com/spiral/main.html
We perceive time in units: a second lasts the same length of time for everyone. Yet sometimes I have the feeling that time passes faster than usual; sometimes slower, as if I have my own unit of time, based on my perception and sense of it. I asked people to count to ten with their eyes closed and created their unique clocks running on their units of time. Each one is different.
  In other words, time is relative.
