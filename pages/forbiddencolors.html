Forbidden Colors
Next Project
video, book, 2020
full video here
The song “Merry Christmas Mr. Lawrence” is also called “Forbidden Colors”—colors that do not appear in ordinary visual functioning, such as “reddish green”.
 I found myself intrigued by the very idea of visualizing the song “Forbidden Colors” using only colors, somewhat like an oxymoron. I assigned different colors to different notes, and translated the music score into a video of color combinations. By exporting the video as image sequences, I printed out a book, the edges of which demonstrate how time moves through the forbidden colors.
