Vein
Next Project
typeface, 2021
Vein is literally constructed from the white veins of the mandarin I was peeling when I was thinking about how I could make a typeface that is fragile and lively at the same time for my The Crack project.
 I think the vein and the crack share the resemblance of creating the tension between vulnerability and resilience, and they look similar visually.
