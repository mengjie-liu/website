The Yale School of Art hosts its annual graduate open studios virtually for the first time in 2021, with work presented online from the departments of Graphic Design, Painting and Printmaking, Photography, and Sculpture.
         In an effort to realize an authentic digital representation of what is traditionally an intimate experience with each MFA students' work and practice, many students have chosen to share drop-in "Live on Zoom" hours, allowing members of the public to connect directly to the artists and their studio spaces. Open Studios 2021 also features a special series of virtual student-involved programming throughout the weekend.
