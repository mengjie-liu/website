     Time proceeds as if walking down an endless spiral.
     Twenty-four hours constitute one day, seven days one week, four weeks one month, twelve months one year. Time is divided, by human beings, with all these units we've invented. Tough time moves forward along a straight line, it is these units that bend the line into a spiral, creating an illusion of circularity.
     This work consists of four spirals, each representing a temporal unit mentioned above. ‘Day Spiral’ consists of twenty-four revolutions,‘Week Spiral’ seven, ‘Month Spiral’ four, and ‘Year Spiral’ twelve. Through dragging, zooming the model, and observing it from different perspectives, you are invited to rethink what time is.
